> • Slash to scout. A satisfying fruit-ninja-style mini-game with
>   meaningful rewards.

>   tactics.
> • Play the season. Weekly fixtures feed a real league table and a
>   trophy chase.

>   celebrations — never stat boosts.

> the trophy.
