# Privacy notice (placeholder)

This is a placeholder used during alpha/beta. The final policy must be
reviewed by counsel and hosted at the URL referenced in
`APP_STORE_METADATA.md` before production submission.

## What we collect
- A device-generated anonymous **install id**. We never collect your
  name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.
- A **cohort tag** chosen by the build (e.g. `alpha-wave-1`). You can
  change or clear this from Settings.
- **Gameplay analytics** — events such as `app_open`, `slash_run_started`,
  `match_completed`. These never include personal data.
- **Crash and error reports** — stack traces and device class only.
- **Feedback you choose to send** through Settings → Send feedback /
  Report an issue, including any text you write.

## What we don't do
- We do not sell your data.
- We do not show third-party ads.
- We do not use loot boxes, gambling, or random monetization.
- We do not share data with brokers or advertising networks.

## How long we keep it
- Install id and gameplay analytics: up to 24 months.
- Crash reports: up to 12 months.
- Feedback: up to 24 months or until resolved.

## Your choices
- Reset progress at any time from Settings → Account.
- Send a deletion request to support@slash-football.example with your
  install id (visible in Settings → Tester profile).

## Children
Slash Football is rated 4+ / Everyone. We do not knowingly collect
information from children under 13. Parents who believe a child has
provided data should contact support to delete it.
