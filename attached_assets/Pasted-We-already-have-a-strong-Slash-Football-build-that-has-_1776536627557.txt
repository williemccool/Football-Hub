We already have a strong Slash Football build that has gone through feature buildout, production hardening, and alpha-readiness work.

Do NOT rebuild the app.

Do NOT bloat the product.

Do NOT add random major gameplay modes.

Important constraint:

Keep everything portable and vendor-neutral. Do not introduce hard dependency on Replit-specific services, SDKs, or operational assumptions. The codebase must remain easy to move later to another editor, repo, backend, CI/CD flow, and deployment stack.

# GOAL

This phase has two objectives:

1. Audit and complete any remaining gaps from the alpha-readiness phase

2. Make Slash Football ready for beta / soft launch / production-grade v1 release preparation

This phase should focus on:

- launch monetization structure

- launch operations readiness

- store/beta readiness

- final KPI instrumentation and experimentability

- cosmetics-driven revenue foundation

- release-candidate polish

Keep the product true to the original philosophy:

- no pay-to-win at launch

- cosmetics-first monetization

- progression must stay fair

- slash loop and club-building loop remain the heart of the game

# PART 2 — MONETIZATION FOUNDATION (NO PAY-TO-WIN)

Build the monetization foundation for v1, but keep the game fair.

## 2A. Cosmetics-first shop expansion

Take the placeholder cosmetics screen and make it feel real and launch-ready.

Support cosmetic categories:

- kits

- crests

- pitch themes

- goal celebration effects

- UI themes if appropriate

- club banners / frames / profile flair if appropriate

Requirements:

- preview states

- owned vs locked states

- equip flow

- rarity presentation

- pricing placeholder model

- no real payment integration yet unless already safely scaffolded

- clean extensible product model

## 2B. Soft currency vs premium currency structure

Design a clean portable currency model for launch.

Suggested structure:

- Coins = soft progression currency

- Premium cosmetic currency = optional premium currency used only for cosmetics/pass if needed

- Essence / fragments / catalysts remain progression currencies, not monetization shortcuts

Requirements:

- no pay-to-win mechanics

- no paid direct rating boosts

- no monetized unfair power acceleration in competitive loop

- architecture should support both direct cosmetic purchase and pass rewards

## 2C. Season pass structure

Add the structural foundation for a launch season pass.

Support:

- free reward track

- premium reward track placeholder

- season XP linkage

- claim flow

- cosmetics-forward reward design

- some fair progression rewards may exist, but keep them non-abusive and balanced

Requirements:

- keep implementation modular

- no forced payment integration yet if not ready

- make it easy to turn on later

## 2D. Store product model

Create a portable product/catalog model for:

- cosmetics

- bundles

- season pass

- limited-time offers

- future founder pack / starter pack

Do not hardcode product definitions into UI components.

Use structured config/models.
